# e-Plant Shopping
An e-commerce web app for discovering, buying, and managing indoor & outdoor plants, planters, and care accessories. Built for performance, accessibility, and a delightful shopping experience.

## ‚ú® Key Features
- **Product Catalog** with categories (Indoor, Outdoor, Succulents, Herbs, Accessories)
- **Advanced Search & Filters** (price range, light level, size, toxicity, pet-safe)
- **Product Details** with images, care instructions, and related items
- **Cart & Checkout** with guest checkout and discount codes
- **User Accounts**: sign up/sign in, profile, order history, wishlists
- **Admin Dashboard**: manage products, inventory, orders, and promotions
- **Reviews & Ratings** with moderation tools
- **Inventory & Stock Control** with low-stock alerts
- **Shipping & Tax Calculation** (pluggable providers)
- **Secure Payments** (Stripe by default, easily swappable)
- **Email Notifications** (order confirmations, shipment updates)
- **Internationalization (i18n)** and **currency formatting**
- **Responsive UI** (mobile-first), **dark mode**, and **accessibility (WCAG AA)**
